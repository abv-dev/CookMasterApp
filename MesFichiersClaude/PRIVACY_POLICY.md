# Privacy Policy for CookMaster

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to CookMaster ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using CookMaster, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

When you use CookMaster, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

**Cooking Data:**
- Cooking history (meat types, cooking methods, times, temperatures)
- Personal notes and ratings
- Favorite recipes
- User preferences (units, language)

**User Content:**
- Photos you upload of your dishes (optional)
- Comments and feedback

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data:**
- App features you use
- Frequency and duration of use
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- IP address (anonymous)
- Crash reports and error logs

**Device Information:**
- Device model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

### 3. Information We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:
- Your precise location
- Contacts from your device
- Microphone data (unless you use voice commands, with your permission)
- Photos without your explicit permission
- Any payment information (app is free)

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- **HTTPS:** All data transmitted over secure connections
- **Access Control:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits:** Periodic security reviews

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Accounts inactive for 3+ years may be deleted
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

**Access:** Request a copy of your data
**Correction:** Update inaccurate information
**Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
**Portability:** Export your cooking history
**Opt-out:** Disable analytics and non-essential notifications

### How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise these rights by:
1. Going to **Settings** > **Privacy** in the app
2. Emailing us at: [privacy@cookmaster.app]
3. We will respond within 30 days

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to be forgotten

- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent
